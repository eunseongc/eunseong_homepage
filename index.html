Eunseong Choi

I am a Ph.D. candidate at Sungkyunkwan University in the DIAL research group. My research focuses on Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) to enhance user access to information through advanced AI technologies. If you have any questions about my research or other topics, please feel free to contact me.
